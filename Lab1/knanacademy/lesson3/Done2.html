Recipe #1: Classic Fried Rice

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 2 cups
Eggs 2
Soy sauce 2 tablespoons
Vegetables (carrot, peas, etc.) 1 cup
Sesame oil 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok.
Step 2: Scramble the eggs and set aside.
Step 3: Saut√© vegetables until tender.
Step 4: Add the cooked rice, scrambled eggs, and soy sauce. Stir well.
Step 5: Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve hot.

Recipe #2: Rice Pudding

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 1 cup
Milk 2 cups
Sugar 1/3 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1: Combine rice, milk, and sugar in a pot.
Step 2: Simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
Step 3: Add vanilla extract and cinnamon. Stir well.
Step 4: Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Serve warm or chilled.
